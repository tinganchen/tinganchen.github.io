    About Me

    I am a Ph.D. Student at National Taiwan University, Taiwan advised by Prof. Ming-Syan Chen. I am also a research assistant at Institute of Information Science, Academia Sinica, Taiwan. My research interests include computer vision, machine learning, and statistical learning. I was/am working on efficient learning (quantization, structured pruning, and federated learning) on vision and language tasks.
